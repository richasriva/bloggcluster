For investing in cryptocurrency, the documents needed are any proof of your residence in the country in which you are currently residing such as a PAN Card and Aadhar Card in India.

You can prefer the CoinDCX app, Binance, CoinSwitch Kuber App, or WazirX for investing in cryptocurrency.

Steps to invest in Cryptomarket:-

1. Download the cryptocurrency app such as Binance, CoinDCX, CoinSwitch Kuber, WazirX, etc..

2. Sign up with your google account and fill the further information such as name, email-id, and phone number.

3. The app will then verify your Aadhaar Card and Pan Card.

4. After verifying, you have to enter your account details through which you are going to invest in cryptocurrencies.

5. Now, that you are done with your KYC, the app will create a bank account for you in the Crypto exchange market.

6. Now, you can buy any cryptocurrency such as Bitcoin, Doge, Cardano, etc, etc.,

Well, this was about how to invest in cryptocurrencies, but do you know you can earn also in the crypto market? Following are the ways with the help which you can earn in the crypto market.

1. Referral and coupons: - Almost all crypto apps these days are promoting their referral program in which you can earn money up to 400rs referral in India by sharing their app with your friends and once your friend created their account the money in the form of cryptocurrency got deposited in your wallet. You can exchange this money in the form of cash by instantly selling those received amounts of crypto.

2. Buy and Hold: - Buying stable cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc., holding it for a specific duration of time such as 6 months, 1 year, and then selling it can be profitable depending upon the rate of the cryptocurrency in future.

3. Daily trading in the Crypto market: - You can also earn money by trading the crypto daily, but for that, you must have proper knowledge about trading otherwise it could be riskier for you. Most of the time daily skilled traders make a huge amount of money from their capital.

4. Staking your cryptocurrency: - You can also earn good interest on your capital by locking your cryptocurrencies in such banks or apps like Coin DCX which promise you to give a certain interest on your capital after a specific duration of time.
