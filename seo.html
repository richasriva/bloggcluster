Learn SEO and optimize your website.

SEO is also known as Search Engine Optimization and it is the process of enhancing the performance of your website and helping it to get ranked on google search engine pages without spending money on ads.

In this blog, you will be able to know the basics of SEO which can optimize your website and help your website to rank.

1.Very first step of SEO is that you have to submit your site along with the sitemap to google so that the crawler of google can crawl to your website and could index it according to your site map. You can make your sitemap in a few seconds with the help of any informative YouTube videos.

2.Register to the Google Search Console to see that how often your site appears in the google console and which those keywords of your content are helping the google traffic to visit your website.

3. Keyword research is another parameter on which your website can rank, for this you can use keyword research tools like www.keywordplanner.com, SEO tools like SEMrush, etc. Writing your content according to the keyword is one of the main steps of SEO. Always keep your content interconnected to that of the keyword.

There are mainly two types of keywords long-tailed and short-tailed keywords, you can search your keywords on google or some keyword-related free site easily.

4.Clear and healthy content: - Clear and healthy content plays an important part in SEO. Make sure that the title, body, and conclusion of your content are interconnected to each other. Otherwise, it will bore your user, and this will affect the ranking of your website.

5.Apart from these, the ranking and SEO of your website mainly depend upon the ON-Page SEO, OFF-Page SEO, and technical SEO. Letâ€™s learn it briefly.

6.ON- Page SEO : - Optimizing your title tags in your website HTML file, optimizing the meta description of your site, optimizing the URL of the site, optimizing heading, optimizing images, and adding other backlinks to your website are some of the SEO main on-page techniques. You can more read about this on any google blog or can wait for bloggcluster to upload it.

7.OFF-Page SEO: - Building links for your website is off-page SEO for example:- how much social media share your website is getting, how much you are doing marketing for your content, and some local SEO like optimizing your page based on the local search result analytics and local traffic.

8.Technical SEO:- This SEO is related to the technology, for example, How your site is appearing to the users, Whether it is easy to surf, is your website secure with an SSL certificate, Is the content published by your website is unique, etc.
